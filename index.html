FOCUS AREAS AT KYEG

Health Awareness

Our healthcare initiatives encompass health clinics, gender empowerment workshops, and health campaigns to promote awareness and access to basic healthcare services, ensuring holistic well-being.

Education Support

We provide educational support through scholarships, mentorship, and talent development programs, fostering creativity and self-expression through cultural and artistic activities.

Economic Empowerment

We aim for economic empowerment through a saving and credit scheme, business and financial literacy, vocational training, and community projects fostering talent and entrepreneurship.
