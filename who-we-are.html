WHO WE ARE

KIKAAYA YOUTH EMPOWERMENT GROUP (KYEG) is a community based organization aiming at empowering the youth and young women. The organization responds to the challenges of youth and young women, including but not limited to poverty, limited access to education, unemployment, sexual reproductive health rights, poor sanitation, exposure to drug addiction, peer influence and crime, through a holistic supportive approach that encompasses economic empowerment, skilling, education access, talent development, mentorship, gender equality, health awareness, and environmental conservation.

Mission: To improve the quality of life of young people through positive youth development in a safe and supportive environment to become active and productive members of their communities, schools and families.
Vision: To create vibrant and prosperous communities where young people actively participate in the decisions that affect their everyday lives in order to become positive agents of change.
Goal: To foster and develop programs for increased learning and growth to help the youth and young women to build self-confidence, self-esteem and self-awareness as they pursue their dreams.
Slogan: Empowering Youth, Transforming Communities.

Empowering youth and young women through our supportive system of economic empowerment, education access, health awareness and environmental conservation.
