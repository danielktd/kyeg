Executive Summary

PROJECT INFLUENCE is initiative organised by Kikaaya Youth Empowerement Group- KYEG aimed at engaging adolescents and youth in secondary schools, in dance competitions to raise the awareness on health and wellness.

Through the universal language of dance set to take effect February 2025, we aim to inspire positive behavioral change, promote health education, and foster a sense of unity among the youth while addressing critical challenges like mental health. This project will not only empower the participants but also contribute to a healthier and more informed youth population.

Problem Statement

Adolescents and youth continue to face significant challenges related to health awareness, including limited access to information and resources leading to preventable health issues. The lack of engaging and informative health programs hinders their ability to make informed choices about their well-being.
Our target community is Secondary Schools in Kawempe Division.


Goals

  • To reach out and impact over 1,000 adolescents and youth within this community.

  • Raise health awareness and education among adolescents and youth within this communities.

  • Promote physical fitness and mental well-being through dance and creative expression.

  • Encourage youth to take an active role in their health and that of their communities.

  • Foster a sense of unity and togetherness among participants.

Vision

To create a generation of empowered, informed and healthy youth who will lead positive change in their communities.

Mission

To engage and inspire adolescents and youth in Kampala's slum communities through dance competitions, using the power of movement and creativity to instill health awareness, encourage active lifestyles and build a strong sense of community.
