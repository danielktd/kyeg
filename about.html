WHO WE ARE

KIKAAYA YOUTH EMPOWERMENT GROUP (KYEG) is a community based organization that focuses on empowering the youth and young women. The organization shall respond to the challenges of youth and women, including but not limited to poverty, limited access to education, unemployment, sexual reproductive health rights, poor sanitation, exposure to drug addiction, peer influence and crime, through a holistic supportive approach that encompasses economic empowerment, skilling, education access, talent development, mentorship, gender equality, health awareness, and environmental conservation.

Mission: To improve the quality of life of young people through positive youth development in a safe and supportive environment to become active and productive members of their communities, schools and families.
Vision: To create vibrant and prosperous communities where young people actively participate in the decisions that affect their everyday lives in order to become positive agents of change.
Goal: To foster and develop programs for increased learning and growth to help the youth and young women to build self-confidence, self-esteem and self-awareness as they pursue their dreams.
Slogan: Empowering Youth, Transforming Communities.
OBJECTIVES AT KYEG

Explore our key objectives aimed at empowering youth and young women through education, health, economic opportunities, and environmental advocacy.

Education

To Increase access and support to quality education for youth and young women through scholarships, provision of scholastic materials and other academic support.

Economic Empowerment

To encourage the youth and young women to engage in savings and credit schemes.

Entrepreneurship

To equip youth and young women with practical skills for empowerment through vocational training and entrepreneurial programs.

Health

To promote health awareness and provide access to healthcare services, including prevention of HIV and mental health support for the physical and emotional well-being of the youth and young women.

Environment

To create awareness and engage the youth and young women to champion the campaigns on environmental conservation and climate change.

Core Values At Kyeg

KYEG, has fundamental beliefs and principles that guide the actions, decisions and behaviors of the organization and its members. These values serve as compass, shaping the organization`s culture, goals, and interactions with the communities it serves:

Excellence

We strive for the highest quality in all our programs and services, therefore all members shall uphold commitment to achieving excellence in all their endeavors.

Empowerment

We believe in the inherent potential of every youth and aim to empower them to reach their full potential and to take control of their lives.

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our activities and relationships.

Collaboration

We recognize the strength of unity; therefore work in partnership with local communities, organizations and stakeholders to maximize our impact.

Accountability

We are responsible and answerable for our actions, decisions and results to foster transparency.

FOCUS AREAS AT KYEG

Our focus areas determine the nature of activities we handle.

Economic Empowerment

We strive for economic empowerment through various avenues, including a savings and credit scheme, business and financial literacy programs, vocational training initiatives, and community projects geared towards nurturing talent and fostering entrepreneurship.

Education Support

We offer comprehensive educational support, including scholarships, mentorship opportunities, and talent development programs. Through these initiatives, we aim to nurture creativity and self-expression among individuals, fostering personal growth and community engagement.


Health Awareness

Our healthcare initiatives encompass health clinics, gender empowerment workshops, and health campaigns to promote awareness and access to basic healthcare services, ensuring holistic well-being.

Environment Conservation

Our environmental conservation efforts encompass waste management projects, recycling programs, reduction in plastic usage, and tree planting campaigns, all aimed at promoting sustainability and ensuring a healthier future.
